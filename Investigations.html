The main purpose of this cite is to be a onestop for information for those seeking to uncover mysteries.

The intent of this particular tab is that after fostering a fairly loyal and good sized fanbase, introduce investigations. Investigations will work as following, people vote for topics they wish to see investigated further, depending on the number of votes and requests a max of 5 investigations will be sponsered at a time (sponsered as in the website/ app openly encourages and advertises the investigation taking place) after a week or 2 the information will be gathered and reported and released.
