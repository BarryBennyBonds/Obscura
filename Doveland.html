Doveland, Wisconsin

Doveland, Wisconsin was supposedly a small town that disappeared in the 1990s. Some people in Wisconsin alledgy posses shirts,mugs, and souvenirs from the towns with people going as far as to say they remember the town or had family who lived there.When searching the town on Google there existed a 'Ghost trace' of Doveland with recommended search options that brought up no results. Similarities between Doveland is also seen other supposed small towns across the United States, such as Urkhammer Iowa which was said to have disappeared without a trace in 1928, and Langville, Montana.
